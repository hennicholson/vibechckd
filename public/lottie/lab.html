How to use 
• Each tile plays one Lottie. Scrub the per-tile slider to inspect any frame.
• Play / Pause / Restart at the top affects all 4 at once for side-by-side review.
• Speed slider re-applies on next play.
• "Reload JSON" re-fetches each Lottie from disk — useful after re-running
  scripts/gen-tab-lotties.mjs to iterate on the JSON without a page reload.
• Drop notes in the corresponding section of vibechckd.md when you've nailed
  the animation you want to keep.
